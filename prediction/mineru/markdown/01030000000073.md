In this content, DPN Argentina provides a brief explanation of the SDGs and the 2030 Agenda action plans, and most importantly, their role in advancing the 2030 Agenda through the SDGs Monitoring and Evaluation Program with a focus on certain thematic areas. These focuses allow DPN Argentina to investigate through monitoring and preparing reports on the development of public policies and actions of organizations responsible for compliance with the SDGs, as well as proposals, and recommendations to strengthen related processes.
